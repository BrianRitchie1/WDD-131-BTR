Purpose

This website will be a place for people who enjoy memes and who seek for find the orgins of these internet jokes. I hope this will be educational an entertaings at the same time.

Audience

My audience will likely be a bunch of young people you enjoy memes as well as old people who want to know what their children are spouting off.

Dynamic elements

The things that are dynamic on my final site are going to be the meme chooser on the main page, which will allow one to choose any meme that they want, provided they know the number. The second page will have a sort by number and alphebetical feature, which will be more helpful if you dont know the number of the meme.
